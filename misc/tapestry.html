Colonialism WHEN PLAYED Choose a territory tile you control and gain each of the following resources based on its land terrain(s): mountain ([WORKER]), grassland ([CULTURE]), forest ([FOOD]), desert ([COIN]). Void if you do not have controlled TILES Only works on territory *tiles*, cannot be used on pre-printed territory hexes.
Dark Ages WHEN PLAYED Regress once on 3 different advancement tracks if possible, then advance three times on the remaining track. Do not gain any benefits or bonuses. Pick Landmarks on track where advancing. Cannot select track blocked by Dictatorship. Can select track where advance won’t resolve fully or finished. If you are on the beginning of a track, you can still use that as "regressing" without actually moving the token.
Democracy WHEN PLAYED Draw 3 [TAPESTRY], then discard any number of [TAPESTRY] from your hand to gain <<2>> each.

Dictatorship WHEN PLAYED Advance on any track and gain the benefit (you may pay to gain the bonus). Opponents may not advance on that track until after your next turn. Effect ends immediately if this is your last turn. Cannot select track blocked by Dictatorship (copy). Can select track where maxed out. You cannot CHOOSE not to take a benefit, but are not otherwise prevented from taking the advancement if unable to do so
Diplomacy WHEN PLAYED Each player gains [RESOURCE]. You also gain 1 building from your income mat. Second part is void if no buildings left
Dystopia WHEN PLAYED Gain any landmark from the landmark board and place it in your capital city. Not include tech landmarks. Void if no more landmarks.
